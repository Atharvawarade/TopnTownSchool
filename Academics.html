Our Academic Programs

At Kids School, we offer academic programs from Nursery to 8th grade, providing a comprehensive learning journey for our students.

We provide:

  • Nursery & Kindergarten: A nurturing environment for young learners to explore, play, and develop essential skills through hands-on activities and guided learning experiences.
  • Primary School (1st to 5th Grade): Foundational education focusing on literacy, numeracy, science, and arts, with an emphasis on fostering curiosity and creativity.
  • Middle School (6th to 8th Grade): Advanced curriculum designed to deepen students' understanding of core subjects while preparing them for the challenges of high school and beyond.

Our dedicated faculty members are committed to providing a supportive and engaging learning environment, where every child can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.
