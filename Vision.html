There are many schools operating in every city in the country. All schools declare themselves as the number one school in the city and district, and continue to do so. Despite the lack of seating arrangements in classrooms, admissions are ongoing under the adjust culture. A learned teacher can only manage large groups of a hundred or two hundred students. This does not bring about the desired qualitative and positive change in the student's behavior.

In such a situation, the "Dhruv Foundation Trust" has started a mission to innovate in the field of education by operating twenty-seater schools. Under this mission, schools are envisioned where each section of a class will only have 20 students. Consequently, a teacher can provide the prescribed subject matter well in their class, and students can comprehend the given knowledge effectively. The foundation is working to prepare a team that can give wings to this mission. In the future, the mission is planned to be expanded through franchise branches not only in Dholpur city but also in other cities of Rajasthan. We are committed to starting a new initiative in the field of education by providing our curriculum material and other child-centric educational content and material to Twenty Seater Schools (Top N' Town).

As the first step, we have started with the "Top N' Town 20-20 School" in Hundawal Nagar Phase 2, Dholpur city. In 2018, Dhruv Foundation began an English medium school from Play Group to Class 8 for elementary education. Although the pace slowed down due to the Corona pandemic in 2019, positive results are now gradually starting to appear.

Let's move forward together and dedicate our contribution to the nation through education.
